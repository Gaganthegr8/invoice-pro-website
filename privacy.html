Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

This policy covers two separate things: this website (freeinvoice4u.com) and the Invoice Pro desktop application. They handle data very differently, so we've kept them clearly separated below.

The Invoice Pro desktop app

Invoice Pro is designed to run entirely offline. The app does not have a server, does not require an account, and does not transmit your invoices, client details, or company information anywhere.

  • All data (invoices, clients, companies, departments, settings) is stored locally on your computer.
  • The app does not phone home, does not sync to any cloud service, and does not share data with us or any third party.
  • You are responsible for backing up your own data — the app includes a "Backup Data" button for this purpose.

This website (freeinvoice4u.com)

Unlike the app, this website does collect limited data, used only to understand traffic and improve the site:

Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics (GA4) to understand how visitors use this site — pages viewed, time on site, general location (country/city level), device type, and which download button was clicked. This data is aggregated and anonymized by Google and is not used to personally identify you. You can opt out using the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Google AdSense

This site may display ads served by Google AdSense. Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites. You can learn more about how Google uses data and manage your ad personalization settings at Google's Ads Policy page and opt out of personalized advertising via Google Ads Settings.

Cookies

This site uses cookies set by Google Analytics and Google AdSense for the purposes described above. We do not set any first-party tracking cookies of our own.

Data we do not collect

We do not collect your name, email, payment details, or any business data through this website. The contact link in our FAQ uses your own email client (mailto:) and any message you choose to send is between you and us directly — it is not logged by this site.

Children's privacy

This website and app are intended for business use and are not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to support@freeinvoice4u.com.
